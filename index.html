Common Questions & Dental Education

Frequently Asked Questions

We accept most major insurance plans including Delta Dental, Cigna, Aetna, BlueCross BlueShield, and many others. Our office staff will verify your benefits before your appointment and help maximize your coverage. We also offer payment plans for those without insurance.

Arrive 15 minutes early to complete any paperwork. Bring your ID, insurance card, and a list of current medications. Brush and floss before your appointment, but avoid heavy meals. If you're anxious, let us know in advance so we can ensure your comfort.

Most patients benefit from checkups and cleanings every six months. However, some conditions may require more frequent visits. During your examination, we'll recommend a personalized schedule based on your oral health needs.

Call our office immediately at (123) 456-7890. For a knocked-out tooth, keep it moist (milk or saliva) and see us within 30 minutes. For a cracked tooth, rinse with warm water and apply a cold compress. For toothaches, take OTC pain relievers and call us for a same-day appointment.
